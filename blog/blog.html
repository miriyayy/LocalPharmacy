Health and Medical Products

The health and medical category offers a variety of products to assist you in your daily care. These products are designed to alleviate or manage health issues and can improve your quality of life when used correctly.

Intimate Gels: Specially formulated for sensitive areas, intimate gels provide hygienic cleaning and can help reduce various sensitivities. They are particularly important for women’s personal care. It is essential to consult your doctor and/or pharmacist before using these products.

Adult Diapers and Underpads: In old age, issues such as urinary incontinence or limited mobility are common and can affect daily life, comfort, and self-confidence. Adult diapers offer great convenience for managing these conditions. They absorb leaks and unwanted odors, helping maintain hygiene for elderly individuals.

Underpads, on the other hand, play a significant role in cleanliness and hygiene. They allow for the hygienic cleaning of elderly individuals, making life easier for both caregivers and the elderly themselves. These products contribute to a more comfortable and healthy lifestyle for elderly individuals.

Breast Pumps: Essential for breastfeeding mothers, breast pumps facilitate milk expression and can help increase milk production. They allow mothers to regularly pump milk, especially when breastfeeding is not possible or challenging. Additionally, breast pumps help mothers store milk for later use, ensuring continuous breastfeeding and supporting the baby’s healthy growth.

Corn Products: These are used to alleviate or treat corns on the feet. By choosing the right product, you can get relief from corns.

Thermometers: Thermometers are used to measure body temperature and play an essential role in monitoring illnesses. With a reliable and accurate thermometer, you can easily track your body temperature.

These are just a few of the frequently used products in the health and medical category. Obtaining these products from reliable sources can best support your healthcare needs. When selecting the products you need to maintain and improve your health, be cautious and seek expert advice.

The information provided here is not a substitute for a doctor’s or pharmacist’s advice and is not intended for treatment purposes. To find the most suitable product for your health, please consult your doctor and/or pharmacist.

Skincare in Your 30s

Entering your 30s marks a period when your skin's needs begin to change, showing the first signs of aging. Fine lines and a duller complexion might become noticeable, but with the right care, your skin can retain its glow.

Understanding Skin Changes: By your 30s, collagen production decreases, and environmental factors like sun exposure and pollution start to take a toll. This can lead to uneven skin tone, fine lines, and dryness.

La Roche Posay Effaclar Gel: A cleansing gel designed for oily and acne-prone skin. It gently removes dirt and sebum, leaving the skin feeling refreshed and clean.

WIWIFY Moisturizing Cream: Formulated to provide intense hydration, this water-based cream nourishes the skin barrier and reduces the appearance of fine lines.

Hydration is Key: No matter your skin type, hydration is essential. Products containing hyaluronic acid and ceramides can help maintain your skin's moisture balance. Morning routines can include light moisturizers, while nighttime care can focus on richer, nourishing formulas.

CeraVe Moisturizing Cream: Enriched with ceramides and hyaluronic acid, this cream offers long-lasting hydration suitable for sensitive skin.

Avene Night Cream: Provides overnight hydration and comfort with its lightweight, non-greasy formula infused with Avene Thermal Water.

Remember, your skin is one of your most valuable assets. Take the time to understand its needs and provide it with the care it deserves.

Natural Makeup Trend: "No-Makeup Makeup" Look

November 7, 2024

The "No-Makeup Makeup" trend emphasizes natural beauty, offering a fresh and radiant appearance. The goal is to enhance the skin's natural glow with minimal products, achieving a smoother and more natural look.

1. Prep Your Skin: Hydration is Key

A well-moisturized skin is the foundation of flawless makeup. Using an effective moisturizer suited to your skin type ensures your makeup blends seamlessly and stays radiant throughout the day.

Avene Cicalfate Repair Cream: Helps hydrate sensitive skin and strengthens the skin barrier. Apply twice daily on clean, dry skin.

Bioderma Sebium Sensitive Cream: Balances sebum production and smooths skin texture. Massage onto clean skin once or twice daily.

2. Choose a Lightweight Base: BB or CC Cream

Opt for BB or CC creams to naturally even out your skin tone without the heavy feel of foundation. These products provide light coverage and hydration.

Nuxe Prodigieux BB Cream: Delivers a radiant and smooth complexion while providing hydration. Suitable for all skin types.

Uriage Roseliane CC Cream: Protects against UV rays while hydrating and smoothing sensitive skin.

3. Brighten Under-Eye Area

Apply a small amount of concealer to brighten the under-eye area, hide shadows, and achieve a fresh look.

Maybelline Instant Eraser Concealer: Covers fine lines and brightens the under-eye area with ease.

4. Define Natural Brows

Enhance your brows with a pencil or gel in a shade close to your natural brow color for a soft and natural frame.

5. Add a Pop of Color with Blush

Choose cream or liquid blushes for a natural flush. Apply on the cheeks for a youthful, radiant touch.

6. Highlight for a Subtle Glow

Use a highlighter on your cheekbones, nose bridge, and inner eye corners for a luminous finish.

7. Minimal Eye Makeup

Use nude shades on your eyelids and a touch of mascara for soft, defined eyes.

8. Nude Lips

Complete your look with a nude or tinted lip balm for a natural finish.
